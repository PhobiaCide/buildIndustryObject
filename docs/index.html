🏗 EveIndyCalc

🏭 An industry tool for 🚀 Eve Online 🔗

🚧 Under Construction

🌌 Overview 👀 🧊

🚬 phew...

  1. ⌨ The user input:

    1. log in account(s) through the app that have relevant Corp roles and grant any requested permisions.

    2. a build quota - this is the desired types and quantities of items desired to be kept on-hand

    3. the stations(s) in which to run the jobs

  2. The runtime:

    1. monitors inventory - quantities of items in inventory locations filters by any combination(s) of the following:

      1. structures

      2. hangars

      3. specific containers

    2. monitors market sell orders

      1. these items will be removed from the build quota until they have sold

      2. keeps a log of what is sold and at what price for later

    3. monitors market buy orders

      1. these items will be removed from a shopping list

      2. keeps a log of what was bought and at what price for estimation of real profit

    4. 🩸monitors killboard kills

      1. logs data

    5. 💣 monitors killboard losses

      1. ships and modules appearing in killboard lossesare immediately added to the build quota.

      2. logs data for use in making kill board reports

        1. what

  3. The tool will calculate:

    1. 💳 total cost to finish all the industry jobs necessary to zero the quota - This includes both ISK cost and material cost

    2. if it is cheaper to buy the product than to produce it

    3. 🧱 an optimal build sequence for those items - essentially a timeline with operations ordered so as to produce as little downtime as possible

    4. This info so to be displayed on a web app hosted by google's free tier services

      1. this will essientially create a virtual job board which Corp members can look to easily determine what needs to be built.
